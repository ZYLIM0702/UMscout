What is UMscout?
UMscout is an app developed specifically for Universiti Malaya (UM) students, aimed at enhancing communication between lecturers and students regarding important announcements. Here are the key features of UMscout:

·       Notification System: UMscout sends notifications to students about new announcements from their lecturers, helping them stay updated without the need to manually check the university's Spectrum platform.

·       User-Friendly Interface: The app offers a clean and intuitive user interface, making it easy for students to navigate and access their announcements.

·       Spectrum Integration: It features an interface that displays content from the Spectrum portal in a native-like format, allowing students to easily view and download files.

·       Privacy Considerations: Lim Zi Yang, the app's developer, has obtained approval from Universiti Malaya's Department of Information Technology (JTM) to publish UMscout, addressing privacy concerns related to students logging into Spectrum and sharing cookies for crawling announcements.
